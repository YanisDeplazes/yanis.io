**Transgressive Rite** is a collection of visual work documenting the primal energy and experimental spirit of performance art across Europe. Through photography and video, the series captures the boundary-pushing nature of live art, focusing on the intentional and visceral movements that define these unconventional rituals.

This project approaches each artist with an unflinching eye, preserving the intense, ephemeral moments of creative expression.
