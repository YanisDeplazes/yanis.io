Event photography for the 2022 Polyball, the most prestigious public gala at ETH Zurich. With a heritage dating back to the 1880s, the Polyball is recognized as the largest decorated ball in Europe, welcoming approximately 9,000 visitors for a night of classic dance and formal celebration.
